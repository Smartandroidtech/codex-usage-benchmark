Methodology

How we isolated, ran, and measured the comparison between GPT-5.5 medium and GPT-5.6 Sol medium.

8complete suites
96total runs
48runs per model
3deterministic tasks
01 / PROTOCOL

Same task.
Clean context.

Every execution starts in a new thread, uses the same reasoning effort, and must produce an exact, verifiable output.

  1. 01

    Prepare

    Input and expected output are fixed before the run.

  2. 02

    Isolate

    A new process and session are used for each model.

  3. 03

    Execute

    Medium reasoning, same task, no human intervention.

  4. 04

    Measure

    Tokens, cache, time, correctness, and in-run quota.

  5. 05

    Balance

    Model order is reversed in the following phase.

02 / TASKS

Three strictly
deterministic outputs.

The structure reduces output variance without limiting the reasoning work needed to reach it.

A

Aggregate

Structured data aggregation with a prescribed format and expected values.

D

Dependencies

Dependency resolution and ordering with a unique answer.

S

Schedule

Constraint-based scheduling with a predefined output schema.

03 / MEASUREMENT

Quota is read
inside every run.

The primary source is telemetry emitted by the session that just completed the task—not an estimate derived from tokens.

Why isn't the global counter our primary source? Fresh probes showed alternating pools/resets and may include activity outside the benchmark. They remain in the logs as diagnostics, but the verdict uses the in-run counter.

04 / COUNTERBALANCING

Four suites per order.

The design separates the model from the position effect, although it cannot remove the counter's coarse granularity.

05 / LIMITATIONS

What we can say.
And what we cannot.

We can say

The aggregate signal was observed.

Across these 8 suites, GPT-5.6 Sol required 5 more points while producing fewer tokens and finishing faster.

We cannot say

We know the internal billing logic.

The percentage is quantized to integers. The benchmark does not reveal server-side weights, rounding, or accounting rules.

Still to verify

Stability over time.

More five-hour windows across different dates would strengthen the estimate and reveal potential policy changes.
