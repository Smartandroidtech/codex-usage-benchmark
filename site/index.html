THE VERDICT, WITH CAUTION

The evidence is real.
The exact multiplier is not.

Across 8 balanced suites, GPT-5.6 Sol consumed 34 points versus 29. This is a repeated, measurable signal—not proof of the billing mechanism: the counter uses integers and global telemetry may switch reset pools.
